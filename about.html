About Us

Shakarzahi LLC was founded in Texas by three generations of family history, American, and international business executives who collectively have over 30 years of proven business experience to exploit disruptive technologies, while providing innovative infrastructure solutions for communities and worldwide. The company seeks solutions by linking state-of-the-art technologies and Science through key stakeholders, partnerships, and joint ventures with world-class international companies and organizations.

Aziz Shakarhazi

Shakarzahi LLC President and Co-founded Aziz Shakarzahi, CEO, Entrepreneur, and an American philanthropist, brings scientists and technologists together experienced to identify, design, and deliver infrastructure solutions worldwide. It is through these relationships which Shakarzahi LLC brings expertise and resources together. The infrastructure solutions employ breakthrough technologies and six-sigma processes in key sectors to satisfy resource shortages.
